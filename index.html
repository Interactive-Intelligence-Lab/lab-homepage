About

The Interactive Intelligence Research Cluster explores the intersection of human-computer interaction, artificial intelligence, and cognitive science. Our mission is to design intelligent systems that collaborate naturally with humans, augmenting human creativity and decision-making through novel interaction paradigms. We pursue fundamental research in interactive machine learning, multimodal interfaces, and human-AI collaboration, while also building practical systems that bring these ideas into the real world.
